About me

I am a backend developer with over 2 years of experience, while I have been programming for about 8 years. I always strive to create solid code that is easy to maintain and scale. My main skills include developing web and desktop applications, managing databases, implementing integrations with external systems, and creating and updating APIs.

I have had the opportunity to work with various programming languages including Python, C/C++, and PHP, as well as different tools such as Django, Laravel, and React. I am a skilled user of version control systems such as Git and always strive to work according to industry best practices.

I am motivated to continuously improve my skills and am always looking for new challenges. I enjoy working in a team and am ready to help other team members in solving problems. In my free time, I am interested in personal development and learning new technologies.
